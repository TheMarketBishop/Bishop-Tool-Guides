Ask The Assistant

one command, three jobs — all in plain English
/ask question
Your plain-English front door. One command that does three things — answer scanner questions, pull live news, and manage your watchlist — just type what you want. 40 questions a day, answers post in-channel (watchlist actions stay private to you), it shows your remaining count, and it never calls a trade — no entries, exits, or price targets.
e.g. /ask what is IV rank  ·  /ask any news on AMD  ·  /ask add NVDA to my swing watch
Ask about the scanner
Look up a name, filter the board, compare and rank, or learn what a term means — answered from live scanner data only, real names and numbers, nothing invented. Reads the swing windows and live intraday RS.
e.g. /ask strongest intraday semis  ·  /ask bench names with cheap IV clear of earnings
Ask for news
Current events on a name, or why it's moving — answered from the web with sources. The one thing the scanner itself can't tell you.
e.g. /ask any news on NVDA  ·  /ask why is HIMS up today
Manage your watchlist by voice
Add, remove, or check your day and swing watchlists in plain English — each change confirmed with a tap. If a stock isn't in the scanner yet, it's sent for review before it's added. These replies stay private to you.
e.g. /ask add PLTR to my swing watch  ·  /ask remove TSLA  ·  /ask what's on my watchlist

Lookup Commands

pull live data on any ticker
/rs TICKER
The core command. A stock's 252D RS, 20D RS, intraday & weekly RS, liquidity grade, and bench/heating status — with a trend arrow showing if it's strengthening.
e.g. /rs NVDA
/contract TICKER
The highest-volume call and put for the nearest weekly expiry — the contracts actually being traded most right now.
e.g. /contract HIMS
/earnings TICKER
The next earnings date and how many days away it is — so you don't get caught holding into a surprise report.
e.g. /earnings AMD
/bench
The current Active Bench — the strongest long-term + short-term leaders the scanner is tracking right now.
no ticker needed
/heating
The Heating Up list — names whose short-term momentum is accelerating away from their long-term base. The early-ignition radar for potential swing runners.
no ticker needed

Scanner Picks & Analysis

read the whole board, not one name
/leaders count
The strongest names in the whole universe by combined RS (252D + 20D averaged). Ask for up to 25.
e.g. /leaders 15
/recap
Today's bench changes vs the prior day — who joined, who fell off, what's new.
no ticker needed
/compare T1 T2
Two tickers side by side — RS, liquidity, and price, head to head.
e.g. /compare AMD MU
/history TICKER
A stock's 20D RS trend over time and how many days it's held the bench.
e.g. /history PLTR
/sector name
Bare /sector lists every sector bucket you can pick from. Add a name to see the strongest stocks inside that group.
e.g. /sector semis  ·  /sector for the full list
/sectorstrength
Which corner of the market is strongest. Ranks the sector buckets by average combined RS, with a count of bench-grade leaders in each.
no ticker needed

Personal Watchlists

use the commands below, or just talk to /ask

Two watch types — pick the one that matches how you trade

DAYTRADE WATCH /watchday

Pings you when your stock shows up as a strong intraday call candidate — for same-day and short-hold momentum plays. Checked every few minutes during market hours.

triggers: top-20 calls  ·  intraday RS ≥ 90  ·  weekly RS ≥ 60
SWING WATCH /watchswing

Pings you when your stock hits the bench or heating up — the signal that it's become a genuine market leader worth a multi-day swing.

triggers: makes the bench  ·  252D RS ≥ 80  ·  20D RS ≥ 80
/watchday TICKER
Add a stock to your daytrade watch. If it's not in the scanner yet, it's sent for review before it's added. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchday NBIS
/watchswing TICKER
Add a stock to your swing watch. Pings you when it becomes a bench-level leader. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchswing PLTR
/mylist
Shows both your daytrade and swing watchlists in one place.
no ticker needed
/unwatch TICKER
Removes a stock from your watchlists. Use the scope option to clear day, swing, or all at once.
e.g. /unwatch NBIS
/mute
Keep your lists but silence all watch pings temporarily.
no ticker needed
/unmute
Turn your watch pings back on.
no ticker needed

BOT ONLINE

The scanner bot lets you pull live RS ratings, the bench, top option contracts, RS charts, sector strength, and earnings on demand — plus set personal watchlists that ping you the moment your stocks hit the scanner. Type any command in any channel.

You need the Premium Plus role to use the bot. Commands only work inside the server — the bot never responds in DMs.

What's New

newest commands & scan upgrades
/rschart gamma:on
NEW
The RS chart now carries a gamma profile strip — every nearby strike's dealer gamma drawn as a bar beside the price, labeled $strike, $amount. Green = call-heavy, pink = put-heavy, and the brightest bars are the walls. Covers the nearest expiration + nearest monthly, and the chart widens so you can see the gamma levels sitting above and below price — possible targets and brakes at a glance.
The sector board, rebuilt
NEW
Every sector view is now a clean rendered card instead of a wall of text. Type /sector for a menu of all buckets, tap one for its strongest names, then drill straight into an industry — each step its own card. /sectorheatmap gives you a treemap of the whole market and now flips between Sector and Industry level, or zooms into one sector's industries. The ranked views show every RS window at a glance, and empty search boxes now suggest names as you tap — no more guessing what to type.
/feedback
NEW
Tell the Bishop what you want next. Type your idea or issue — the bot's AI may ask one or two quick follow-up questions to sharpen it, then it lands straight in front of the Bishop. Everything stays private to you.
Cleaner top contracts
UPDATED
Daytrade cards no longer pin thin contracts nobody can trade. If a name's top contract has barely any volume, a real, tradeable contract from the next name in line takes the seat instead.
20-day strength flags
NEW
Scan cards now flag names by their 20-day RS — the single strongest edge in the scanner's own tracked history. A 20D+ tag means the name sits in the strength zone where calls have historically hit best; FLAT warns the move may already be asleep.
Faster, more reliable charts
FIXED
Chart commands were occasionally timing out without a reply on heavy builds. That's fixed — the bot now answers instantly and delivers the chart the moment it's ready.

Lookup Commands

pull live data on any ticker
/rs TICKER
The core command. A stock's Long-Term RS (252d), Short-Term RS (20d), intraday & weekly RS, liquidity grade, and bench/heating status — with a trend arrow showing if it's strengthening.
e.g. /rs NVDA
/rschart TICKER GAMMA
A live RS + price chart with short-interval RS bands, now carrying the new gamma profile strip — the dealer walls sitting above and below price.
e.g. /rschart AMD
/contract TICKER
The highest-volume call and put for the nearest weekly expiry — the contracts actually being traded most right now.
e.g. /contract HIMS
/earnings TICKER
The next earnings date and how many days away it is — so you don't get caught holding into a surprise report.
e.g. /earnings AMD
/bench
The current Active Bench — the strongest long-term + short-term leaders the scanner is tracking right now.
no ticker needed
/heating
The Heating Up list — names whose short-term momentum is accelerating away from their long-term base. The early-ignition radar for potential swing runners.
no ticker needed

Scanner Picks & Analysis

read the whole board, not one name
/leaders count
The strongest names in the whole universe by combined RS (LT + ST averaged), now a full ranked card. Use the dropdown to resize the list.
e.g. /leaders 15
/compare T1 T2
Two tickers side by side — every RS window, liquidity, and price, head to head. Dropdown walks the second name through the top leaders.
e.g. /compare AMD MU
/recap
Today's bench changes vs the prior day — who joined, who fell off, what's new.
no ticker needed
/history TICKER
A stock's Short-Term RS trend over time and how many days it's held the bench.
e.g. /history PLTR
/sector name
Bare /sector opens a menu card of every bucket. Pick one for its strongest names, then drill into an industry — all as rendered cards.
e.g. /sector semis  ·  /sector for the menu
/sectorheatmap level sector
A treemap of the whole market by strength across six timeframes. Switch to Industry level or drill into one sector's industries.
e.g. /sectorheatmap level:Industry
/sectorrank
The sector buckets ranked head to head on a clean card, every RS window shown by default.
no ticker needed
/sectorstrength
Which corner of the market is strongest. Ranks the sector buckets by average combined RS, with a count of bench-grade leaders in each.
no ticker needed

Personal Watchlists

get pinged when your stocks hit the scanner

Two watch types — pick the one that matches how you trade

DAYTRADE WATCH /watchday

Pings you when your stock shows up as a strong intraday call candidate — for same-day and short-hold momentum plays. Checked every few minutes during market hours.

triggers: top-20 calls  ·  intraday RS ≥ 90  ·  weekly RS ≥ 60
SWING WATCH /watchswing

Pings you when your stock hits the bench or heating up — the signal that it's become a genuine market leader worth a multi-day swing.

triggers: makes the bench  ·  LT RS ≥ 80  ·  ST RS ≥ 80
/watchday TICKER
Add a stock to your daytrade watch. If it's not in the scanner yet, the bot validates it and adds it automatically. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchday NBIS
/watchswing TICKER
Add a stock to your swing watch. Pings you when it becomes a bench-level leader. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchswing PLTR
/mylist
Shows both your daytrade and swing watchlists in one place.
no ticker needed
/unwatch TICKER
Removes a stock from your watchlists. Use the scope option to clear day, swing, or all at once.
e.g. /unwatch NBIS
/mute
Keep your lists but silence all watch pings temporarily.
no ticker needed
/unmute
Turn your watch pings back on.
no ticker needed

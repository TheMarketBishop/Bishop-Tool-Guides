Bishop's Mind — AI Assistant

ask in plain english

Bishop's Mind is the community's built-in AI. Ask it about any name's RS, the bench, sectors, earnings, live market news, or to pull an RS history chart — and manage your watchlists in plain english ("watch NVDA for swings"). Three ways to reach it:

/ask

Type /ask and your question. The classic way — works in any channel.

@ mention

@Bishop's Mind + your question. Great for charts and quick lookups.

↳ reply

Reply to any of its answers to keep the thread going — it remembers the convo.

It will never tell you to buy, sell, or hold, and never gives price targets. Bishop's Mind reads you the data and the strength — the trade decision is always yours. Fair usage: 50 questions per member per day.

Lookup Commands

pull live data on any ticker
/rs TICKER
The core command. A stock's 252D & 20D RS, intraday & weekly RS, liquidity grade, and bench/heating status — with a trend arrow showing if it's strengthening.
e.g. /rs NVDA
/rschart TICKER NEW
A 10-day RS history chart — daily candles with each day's RS values, plus today's live candle. Add windows for +20D or the full stack.
e.g. /rschart NVDA windows:all
/contract TICKER
The highest-volume call and put for the nearest weekly expiry — the contracts actually being traded most right now.
e.g. /contract HIMS
/earnings TICKER
The next earnings date and how many days away it is — so you don't get caught holding into a surprise report.
e.g. /earnings AMD
/bench
The current Active Bench — the strongest long-term + short-term leaders the scanner is tracking right now.
no ticker needed
/heating
The Heating Up list — names whose short-term momentum is accelerating away from their long-term base. The early-ignition radar for potential swing runners.
no ticker needed

Scanner Picks & Analysis

read the whole board, not one name
/leaders count
The strongest names in the whole universe by combined RS (252D + 20D averaged). Ask for up to 25.
e.g. /leaders 15
/recap
Today's bench changes vs the prior day — who joined, who fell off, what's new.
no ticker needed
/compare T1 T2
Two tickers side by side — RS, liquidity, and price, head to head.
e.g. /compare AMD MU
/history TICKER
A stock's 20D RS trend over time and how many days it's held the bench.
e.g. /history PLTR
/sector name
Bare /sector lists every sector bucket you can pick from. Add a name to see the strongest stocks inside that group.
e.g. /sector semis  ·  /sector for the full list
/sectorstrength
Which corner of the market is strongest. Ranks the sector buckets by average combined RS, with a count of bench-grade leaders in each.
no ticker needed
/sectorof TICKER
Tells you which sector bucket a stock is filed under — handy for finding its peers and reading it against the right group.
e.g. /sectorof NVDA

Personal Watchlists

get pinged when your stocks hit the scanner

Two watch types — pick the one that matches how you trade

DAYTRADE WATCH /watchday

Pings you when your stock shows up as a strong intraday call candidate — for same-day and short-hold momentum plays. Checked every few minutes during market hours.

triggers: strong intraday call setup on the daytrade scan
SWING WATCH /watchswing

Pings you when your stock hits the bench or heating up — the signal that it's become a genuine market leader worth a multi-day swing.

triggers: makes the bench or the heating-up list
/watchday TICKER
Add a stock to your daytrade watch. If it's not in the scanner yet, the bot validates it and adds it automatically. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchday NBIS
/watchswing TICKER
Add a stock to your swing watch. Pings you when it becomes a bench-level leader. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchswing PLTR
/mylist
Shows both your daytrade and swing watchlists in one place.
no ticker needed
/unwatch TICKER
Removes a stock from your watchlists. Use the scope option to clear day, swing, or all at once.
e.g. /unwatch NBIS
/mute
Keep your lists but silence all watch pings temporarily.
no ticker needed
/unmute
Turn your watch pings back on.
no ticker needed

On The Posted Scans

what you'll see on the images — nothing to type

These show up automatically on the scan images the bot posts — read them straight off the picture.

IVR column

Swing scans show an IVR read — green = cheap options, orange = rich premium. A quick check on whether you're overpaying for a breakout.

Earnings badges

Any bench name within about a week of earnings gets flagged — so a multi-day swing doesn't walk into a surprise report.

"1ST SEEN"

Scans mark when a name first showed up — the swing scan carries the date it first made the bench, the daytrade scan the time it first hit today.

Dedicated swing channel

Swing scans post in their own channel, separate from the daytrade scans, so each stream stays clean. Watch it for bench & heating posts.

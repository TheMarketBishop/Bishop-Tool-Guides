Bishop's Mind — AI Assistant

ask the board anything

Bishop's Mind is the scanner's built-in AI. It answers from the live scanner data — it never makes a number up — and it can pull live news when you ask why something's moving. Three ways to reach it:

/ask

Type /ask then your question in any channel. The answer posts publicly so the whole room learns.

@ mention

Tag @Bishop's Mind with a question — same brain, just @ it instead of the slash command.

reply to continue

Reply to any answer to keep the thread going — it remembers what you were just talking about.

Reads the whole board
Ask about any name's RS, the bench, what's heating up, sector strength, or two tickers head-to-head. It pulls the real scanner rows — no guessing.
e.g. what's NVDA's RS · show me the bench · which sector is hottest
Pulls live news
Ask why a name's moving and it fetches current, real news and folds in what the RS is doing — sources included, all in one answer.
e.g. why's ORCL been weak lately
Manages your watchlists
Just say it in plain English — "watch NVDA for swings," "what's on my list," "drop HIMS." It confirms before adding or removing anything.
e.g. add AMD to my day watch
Finds names to track
Ask about a stock the scanner isn't tracking yet and it gets flagged for review — so genuinely tradeable names can get added to the board.
e.g. ask about any optionable name not on the scanner

It never gives buy/sell calls, entries, exits, or price targets — not even as a joke. Bishop's Mind hands you the data and the why; you read the charts and make the trade. That's the whole point.

50 questions per member per day — it tells you how many you've got left. Resets the next day.

Lookup Commands

pull live data on any ticker
/rs TICKER
The core command. A stock's 252D RS & 20D RS, intraday & weekly RS, liquidity grade, and bench/heating status — with a trend arrow showing if it's strengthening.
e.g. /rs NVDA
/contract TICKER
The highest-volume call and put for the nearest weekly expiry — the contracts actually being traded most right now.
e.g. /contract HIMS
/earnings TICKER
The next earnings date and how many days away it is — so you don't get caught holding into a surprise report.
e.g. /earnings AMD
/bench
The current Active Bench — the strongest long-term + short-term leaders the scanner is tracking right now.
no ticker needed
/heating
The Heating Up list — names whose short-term momentum is accelerating away from their long-term base. The early-ignition radar for potential swing runners.
no ticker needed

Scanner Picks & Analysis

read the whole board, not one name
/leaders count
The strongest names in the whole universe by combined RS (252D + 20D averaged). Ask for up to 25.
e.g. /leaders 15
/recap
Today's bench changes vs the prior day — who joined, who fell off, what's new.
no ticker needed
/compare T1 T2
Two tickers side by side — RS, liquidity, and price, head to head.
e.g. /compare AMD MU
/history TICKER
A stock's 20D RS trend over time and how many days it's held the bench.
e.g. /history PLTR
/sector name
Bare /sector lists every sector bucket you can pick from. Add a name to see the strongest stocks inside that group.
e.g. /sector semis  ·  /sector for the full list
/sectorstrength
Which corner of the market is strongest. Ranks the sector buckets by average combined RS, with a count of bench-grade leaders in each.
no ticker needed
/sectorof TICKER NEW
The curated sector bucket(s) a single stock is filed under — e.g. Semis, AI Compute. If it isn't in a specific bucket, you get its broad sector instead.
e.g. /sectorof VRT

Personal Watchlists

get pinged when your stocks hit the scanner

Two watch types — pick the one that matches how you trade

DAYTRADE WATCH /watchday

Pings you when your stock shows up as a strong intraday call candidate — top-of-the-board intraday momentum. For same-day and short-hold plays. Checked every few minutes during market hours.

triggers: strong intraday call candidate on the daytrade board
SWING WATCH /watchswing

Pings you when your stock hits the bench or heating up — the signal that it's become a genuine market leader worth a multi-day swing.

triggers: makes the bench or the heating-up list
/watchday TICKER
Add a stock to your daytrade watch. If it's not in the scanner yet, the bot validates it and adds it automatically. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchday NBIS
/watchswing TICKER
Add a stock to your swing watch. Pings you when it becomes a bench-level leader. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchswing PLTR
/mylist
Shows both your daytrade and swing watchlists in one place.
no ticker needed
/unwatch TICKER
Removes a stock from your watchlists. Use the scope option to clear day, swing, or all at once.
e.g. /unwatch NBIS
/mute
Keep your lists but silence all watch pings temporarily.
no ticker needed
/unmute
Turn your watch pings back on.
no ticker needed

e.g. @Bishop's Mind add HIMS to my swing watch

On The Posted Scans

what you'll see — nothing to type

The scan images that post automatically carry a few built-in reads so you can size up a name at a glance:

"1ST SEEN" columns

The time a name first hit the daytrade board today, and the date it first made the swing bench.

IV Rank (IVR)

Green = cheap options, orange = rich premium. A quick read on whether you're overpaying for the move.

Earnings badges

Swing scans flag any bench name within about a week of earnings — so a multi-day swing doesn't walk into a surprise report.

Dedicated swing channel

Swing scans post in their own channel, separate from the daytrade scans, so each stream stays clean.

Powered by Unusual Whales

Every scan image credits the data source in the footer — the live options, RS, and earnings data all run on the Unusual Whales API.

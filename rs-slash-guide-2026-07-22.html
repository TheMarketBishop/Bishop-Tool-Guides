Lookup Commands

pull live data on any ticker
/rs TICKER
The core command. A stock's Long-Term RS (252d), Short-Term RS (20d), intraday & weekly RS, liquidity grade, and bench/heating status — with a trend arrow showing if it's strengthening.
e.g. /rs NVDA
/contract TICKER
The highest-volume call and put for the nearest weekly expiry — the contracts actually being traded most right now.
e.g. /contract HIMS
/rschart TICKER
An RS chart for the stock — Relative Strength over time with a gamma strip showing the big options positioning. Flip the lookback window right on the chart.
e.g. /rschart NVDA
/earnings TICKER
The next earnings date and how many days away it is — so you don't get caught holding into a surprise report.
e.g. /earnings AMD
/bench
The current Active Bench — the strongest long-term + short-term leaders the scanner is tracking right now.
no ticker needed
/heating
The Heating Up list — names whose short-term momentum is accelerating away from their long-term base. The early-ignition radar for potential swing runners.
no ticker needed

Screener & Presets

build your own filter, then save it
/screen RS filters day % sector
Filter the whole universe your way. Set any RS floors or ceilings you want — 5-minute, 30-minute, 1-hour, intraday, 5-day, 20-day and 252-day — plus a day-move range and a sector or industry. Leave anything blank to ignore it. The bot returns every name that fits.
e.g. /screen d20_min:90 d5_min:80  ·  strong 20-day + 5-day names
/screen preset
Re-run a screen you already saved. Pick it from the preset list and the bot loads those exact filters. Anything you also type is ignored when a preset is loaded.
e.g. /screen preset:my momentum
/presets
See every screen you've saved in one list, and use the delete option to clear one. Up to 10 saved at a time.
e.g. /presets  ·  /presets delete:my momentum

How to save a screen — two taps

1 · BUILD IT /screen

Run /screen with whatever RS floors and sector you want, and look at the results.

2 · SAVE IT Save screen

Like it? Tap the Save screen button under the results, give it a name — done. Reload it any time with /screen preset:.

Scanner Picks & Analysis

read the whole board, not one name
/leaders count
The strongest names in the whole universe by combined RS (LT + ST averaged). Ask for up to 25.
e.g. /leaders 15
/recap
Today's bench changes vs the prior day — who joined, who fell off, what's new.
no ticker needed
/compare T1 T2
Two tickers side by side — RS, liquidity, and price, head to head.
e.g. /compare AMD MU
/history TICKER
A stock's Short-Term RS trend over time and how many days it's held the bench.
e.g. /history PLTR
/sector name
Bare /sector lists every sector bucket you can pick from. Add a name to see the strongest stocks inside that group.
e.g. /sector semis  ·  /sector for the full list
/sectorstrength
Which corner of the market is strongest. Ranks the sector buckets by average combined RS, with a count of bench-grade leaders in each.
no ticker needed
/sectorrank
Ranks the sector and industry groups against each other — a clean visual card of which slice of the market is leading and which is lagging.
no ticker needed
/sectorheatmap
The whole market as a color treemap — hot to cool by strength, across several timeframes at once, so rotation is obvious at a glance.
no ticker needed

Personal Watchlists

get pinged when your stocks hit the scanner

Two watch types — pick the one that matches how you trade

DAYTRADE WATCH /watchday

Pings you when your stock shows up as a strong intraday call candidate — for same-day and short-hold momentum plays. Checked every few minutes during market hours.

triggers: top-20 calls  ·  intraday RS ≥ 90  ·  weekly RS ≥ 60
SWING WATCH /watchswing

Pings you when your stock hits the bench or heating up — the signal that it's become a genuine market leader worth a multi-day swing.

triggers: makes the bench  ·  LT RS ≥ 80  ·  ST RS ≥ 80
/watchday /watchswing mode: Default · Custom
New — set your own trigger levels. Leave the mode on Default and you're pinged on the scanner's own bar (shown above). Switch to Custom and you pick your own RS floors — day watch: intraday / weekly / 20-day; swing watch: long-term / short-term. /mylist shows each name's mode.
/watchday TICKER
Add a stock to your daytrade watch. If it's not in the scanner yet, the bot validates it and adds it automatically. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchday NBIS
/watchswing TICKER
Add a stock to your swing watch. Pings you when it becomes a bench-level leader. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchswing PLTR
/mylist
Shows both your daytrade and swing watchlists in one place.
no ticker needed
/unwatch TICKER
Removes a stock from your watchlists. Use the scope option to clear day, swing, or all at once.
e.g. /unwatch NBIS
/mute
Keep your lists but silence all watch pings temporarily.
no ticker needed
/unmute
Turn your watch pings back on.
no ticker needed

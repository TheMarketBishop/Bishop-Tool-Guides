BOT ONLINE

The scanner bot lets you pull live RS ratings, the bench, top option contracts, earnings, sector leaders, and a daily recap on demand — plus set personal watchlists that ping you the moment your stocks hit the scanner. Type any command in any channel.

Last updated June 29, 2026 — added /recap, /compare, /leaders, /sector, /history, and /unwatch scopes.
You need the Premium Plus role to use the bot. Commands only work inside the server — the bot never responds in DMs.

Lookup Commands

pull live data on any ticker
/rs TICKER
The core command. Shows a stock's Long-Term RS (252 days), Short-Term RS (20 days), intraday & weekly RS, liquidity grade, and whether it's on the bench or heating up — with a trend arrow showing if it's strengthening.
e.g. /rs NVDA
/contract TICKER
Returns the highest-volume call and put for the nearest weekly expiry — the contracts actually being traded most right now.
e.g. /contract HIMS
/earnings TICKER
Shows the next earnings date and how many days away it is — so you don't get caught holding a contract into a surprise report.
e.g. /earnings AMD
/bench
Posts the current Active Bench — the strongest long-term + short-term leaders the scanner is tracking right now.
no ticker needed
/heating
Posts the Heating Up list — names whose short-term momentum is accelerating away from their long-term base. This is the early-ignition radar for potential swing runners.
no ticker needed

Personal Watchlists

get pinged when your stocks hit the scanner

Two watch types — pick the one that matches how you trade

DAYTRADE WATCH /watchday

Pings you when your stock shows up as a strong intraday call candidate — for same-day and short-hold momentum plays. Checked every 5 minutes during market hours.

triggers: top-20 calls  ·  intraday RS ≥ 90  ·  weekly RS ≥ 60
SWING WATCH /watchswing

Pings you when your stock hits the bench or heating up — the signal that it's become a genuine market leader worth a multi-day swing.

triggers: makes the bench  ·  LT RS ≥ 80  ·  ST RS ≥ 80
/watchday TICKER
Add a stock to your daytrade watch. If it's not in the scanner yet, the bot validates it and adds it automatically. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchday NBIS
/watchswing TICKER
Add a stock to your swing watch. Pings you when it becomes a bench-level leader. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchswing PLTR
/mylist
Shows both your daytrade and swing watchlists in one place.
no ticker needed
/unwatch TICKER scope: day|swing|all Updated
Remove a single name with /unwatch TICKER, or clear a whole list with a scope — day, swing, or all. A confirmation button appears before wiping a list so you never clear one by accident.
e.g. /unwatch NBIS · /unwatch scope: all
/mute
Keep your lists but silence all watch pings temporarily.
no ticker needed
/unmute
Turn your watch pings back on.
no ticker needed

Analysis Commands

added today
/recap New
Today's bench changes at a glance. Who's NEW to the bench, who FELL OFF, and who's NEWLY HEATING UP versus yesterday — each with their RS ratings. The fastest way to catch what shifted.
no ticker needed
/compare TICKER1 TICKER2 New
Two names, head to head. Side-by-side LT RS, ST RS, liquidity, price, day move, and bench status. Settles "which one's stronger right now" in one command.
e.g. /compare AMD MU
/leaders count New
The strongest names in the whole market by combined RS — regardless of whether they're on the bench. Your "what's hot right now" pull. Defaults to top 10, up to 25.
e.g. /leaders 15
/history TICKER New
How a name has trended. Its Short-Term RS over recent days plus how many days it's held the bench — staying power versus a one-day pop.
e.g. /history NVDA

Sector Rotation

strongest names by theme
/sector NAME New
The strongest names in a sector, ranked by RS. Perfect for reading rotation — see at a glance whether semis, crypto, or AI compute is leading today. Type the command with no sector name to list every group.
e.g. /sector semis  ·  /sector crypto  ·  /sector ai compute

Look Up A Name

live data on any ticker
/rs TICKER
The core command. A stock's Long-Term RS (252 days), Short-Term RS (20 days), intraday & weekly RS, liquidity grade, and whether it's on the bench or heating up — with a trend arrow showing if it's strengthening.
e.g. /rs NVDA
/contract TICKER
The highest-volume call and put at the nearest weekly expiry — the contracts actually being traded most right now.
e.g. /contract HIMS
/earnings TICKER
The next earnings date and how many days out — so you don't get caught holding a contract into a surprise report.
e.g. /earnings AMD
/compare TICKER TICKER
Two names side by side — LT & ST RS, liquidity, price, day change, bench status. Settle which one's the stronger setup.
e.g. /compare ALAB NVDA
/history TICKER
A stock's Short-Term RS trend over time plus how many days it's held the bench — see whether strength is building or fading.
e.g. /history PLTR

Track Your Own Names

get pinged when your stocks hit the scanner

Two watch types — pick the one that matches how you trade

DAYTRADE WATCH /watchday

Pings you when your stock shows up as a strong intraday call candidate — for same-day and short-hold momentum plays. Checked every few minutes during market hours.

default trigger: intraday RS ≥ 90  ·  weekly RS ≥ 60
SWING WATCH /watchswing

Pings you when your stock hits the bench or heating up — the signal that it's become a genuine market leader worth a multi-day swing.

default trigger: LT RS ≥ 80  ·  ST RS ≥ 80

Set your own strictness updated

By default your watches use the scanner's tuned floors above. Add optional numbers to raise your own bar — you'll only get pinged on names that clear it. Leave them off and nothing changes. Floors are yours alone; they never touch the public scanner, and you can set them anywhere from 30 up.

/watchday NBIS 95 80  →  only pings if intraday RS ≥ 95 AND weekly RS ≥ 80
/watchday TICKER min_intraday min_weekly upd
Add a name to your daytrade watch. Optional RS floors let you tighten the trigger. If it's not in the scanner yet, the bot validates and adds it. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchday NBIS  or  /watchday NBIS 95 80
/watchswing TICKER min_lt min_st upd
Add a name to your swing watch. Optional LT/ST floors tighten the trigger. Pings when it becomes a bench-level leader. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchswing PLTR  or  /watchswing PLTR 88 88
/mylist
Both your daytrade and swing watchlists in one place — including any custom floors you've set.
no ticker needed
/unwatch TICKER scope
Remove one ticker, or clear a whole list with scope: day, swing, or all (asks you to confirm first).
e.g. /unwatch NBIS  or  /unwatch scope:all
/mute
Keep your lists but silence all watch pings temporarily.
no ticker needed
/unmute
Turn your watch pings back on.
no ticker needed

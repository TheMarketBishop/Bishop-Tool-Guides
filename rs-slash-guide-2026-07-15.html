BOT ONLINE

The scanner bot lets you pull live RS ratings, the bench, top option contracts, sector strength and heatmaps, and earnings on demand — set personal watchlists that ping you the moment your stocks hit the scanner, and now tag past breakouts to study the setup that came before the move. Lookup Commands

pull live data on any ticker
/rs TICKER
The core command. A stock's Long-Term RS (252d°), Short-Term RS (20d), plus its live 5-day, 10-day, intraday and 5-minute RS, liquidity grade, and bench/heating status — with a trend arrow showing if it's strengthening.
e.g. /rs NVDA
/contract TICKER
The highest-volume call and put for the nearest weekly expiry — the contracts actually being traded most right now.
e.g. /contract HIMS
/earnings TICKER
The next earnings date and how many days away it is — so you don't get caught holding into a surprise report.
e.g. /earnings AMD
/bench
The current Active Bench — the strongest long-term + short-term leaders the scanner is tracking right now.
no ticker needed
/heating
The Heating Up list — names whose short-term momentum is accelerating away from their long-term base. The early-ignition radar for potential swing runners.
no ticker needed

Breakout Study

learn the setup that came before the move
/breakout TICKER DATE
Tag a stock and the day it broke out. The bot saves what its RS looked like the day before — the setup, not the move. Dates type easy: 7/5/26. Saved to your own list, up to 15.
e.g. /breakout NVDA 7/10/26
/mybreakouts
Posts a card of every name you've tagged — the intraday, 5-day, 10-day and 20-day RS heading into each pop, and the common range across them. Your screener template for the next one.
no ticker needed
/unbreakout TICKER
Removes a stock from your breakout list. Note: the freshest same-day (intraday) read only fills on breakouts tagged within the last few days; older tags still show all three daily reads reaching back about a year.
e.g. /unbreakout NVDA

Personal Watchlists

get pinged when your stocks hit the scanner

Two watch types — pick the one that matches how you trade

DAYTRADE WATCH /watchday

Pings you when your stock shows up as a strong intraday call candidate — for same-day and short-hold momentum plays. Checked every few minutes during market hours.

triggers: top-20 calls  ·  intraday RS ≥ 90  ·  weekly RS ≥ 60
SWING WATCH /watchswing

Pings you when your stock hits the bench or heating up — the signal that it's become a genuine market leader worth a multi-day swing.

triggers: makes the bench  ·  LT RS ≥ 80  ·  ST RS ≥ 80
/watchday TICKER
Add a stock to your daytrade watch. If it's not in the scanner yet, the bot validates it and adds it automatically. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchday NBIS
/watchswing TICKER
Add a stock to your swing watch. Pings you when it becomes a bench-level leader. Up to 15 names.
e.g. /watchswing PLTR
/mylist
Shows both your daytrade and swing watchlists in one place.
no ticker needed
/unwatch TICKER
Removes a stock from your watchlists. Use the scope option to clear day, swing, or all at once.
e.g. /unwatch NBIS
/mute
Keep your lists but silence all watch pings temporarily.
no ticker needed
/unmute
Turn your watch pings back on.
no ticker needed
